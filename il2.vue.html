Welcome to a Lesson on Sweden

From vast forests to beautiful summer meadows, Sweden's nature is iconic. In this lesson, you'll discover the official national animal and the newly chosen national flower that represent the heart of Swedish identity. Let's begin!

Question 1: The National Animal

Often called "Skogens konung" (King of the Forest), this majestic creature is a powerful symbol of Sweden's vast wilderness. Though it was never officially designated, it is overwhelmingly considered the national animal by Swedes.

Question 2: The National Flower

For decades, Sweden had no official national flower. In 2021, a national vote was held to choose one. The winner is a delicate, bell-shaped flower that dots meadows and roadsides, symbolizing the beloved Swedish summer.
