Welcome to a Lesson on Norway

Norway's dramatic landscape of fjords, mountains, and waterfalls is world-famous. In this lesson, we will explore the official national bird and flower that thrive in this stunning environment. Let's begin our journey!

Question 1: The National Bird

This small but incredibly resilient bird is a true Norwegian survivor. It lives by fast-flowing rivers and is unique for its ability to dive and walk on the riverbed to hunt for insects, even during the coldest winters. It was chosen as the national bird in 1963.

Question 2: The National Flower

Norway has two national flowers. This one, chosen by a popular radio poll in 1976, is a hardy plant that covers the coastal heaths and mountainsides in a stunning blanket of purple blooms in late summer. It's a vital source of nectar for bees.
